Hello!

I'm Emily Mikkelsen.

I'm so happy you have made it to my site! I love that you want to get to know more about me before considering booking your photos. One of my favorite parts of taking pictures is getting to meet all of my amazing clients...I love hearing about all of the cool hobbies you have, watch your families interact, and be able to be a part of a moment that we will be able to capture forever!

Although I spend plenty of time behind the lense, I can be found in many other places as well. Between traveling across the PNW watching my two girls play volleyball (Lexi-Carroll College and Olivia-University of Washington), spending hours in the gym or on the field watching my son (Calvin-13 years old) play basketball and football, or finally being able to hang out with my husband, Mac, after our days are finally finished, my favorite place to be is around my people.

In terms of photography, my career has been a little different than a traditional path. I attended WSU pursuing a degree in Psychology (which I have never used! ha!) but got into photography when my girls were little. I loved to capture those cute little blonde girls in pig-tails and matching outfits running around my front yard, and my friends started loving those images too! Slowly I got into a photography career, starting primarily by taking pictures of close family-friends and sports teams that my kids played for, but over time, my awesome friends and family spread my name to many others and through them I've been able to become the photographer I am today!

Overall, one of the things I am most thankful for is that I LOVE what I get to do everyday, and I'd love to get to capture these perfect, fun, crazy moments for you, your team, or your family too :)
